FAQ

What if I change my mind and want to return a product?
No problem! All purchases have a 30-day return window during which you can return the product to get a full refund. Note that this does not apply to any products that have been damaged or tampered with.
Are discounts available on commercial bulk orders?
Absolutely! We offer discounts on the total order cost for any bulk purchases from business and commercial customers. This discount is higher the more you order, allowing you to furnish your office without breaking the bank.
Can I order custom goods that aren't listed in the product catalogue?
Sure! If you have your eye on a specific gadget that you can't find in our selection, just send us an email specifying that product. We will let you know if and when we can make it available for you.
